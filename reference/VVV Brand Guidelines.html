2.3 Construction & clear space
Geometry
Two nested chevrons on a 60° axis. The inner chevron sits at exactly half the outer height — the heritage "VV" read.
Stroke
Single uniform weight with rounded caps and joins. The inner echo holds 55% opacity to signal depth and motion.
Clear space
Minimum margin on every side equals the inner chevron's width. Never crowd the mark with type or graphics.
Minimum size
24px digital / 8mm print. Below this, drop the inner echo for legibility.

05 / Voice & Tone

Operator-grade. No theatre.

VVV talks like a builder who ships, not a fund that pitches. Short. Concrete. Proof over promise.

Direct

Lead with the claim. Cut the throat-clearing. A sentence should survive being read once, fast.

Concrete

Numbers, names, dates. "Backed Kamereo in 2018" beats "deep ecosystem experience."

Confident, not loud

State it plainly and stop. The red does the shouting; the words stay calm.

5.1 Say it / don't say it
✓ On voice
  • "I build the things I invest in."
  • "$15M+ raised. #1 in its category."
  • "One thesis: AI × Vietnam."
  • "Pick one. I'll ship it."
✕ Off voice
  • "Leveraging synergies across the ecosystem."
  • "A diversified portfolio of innovative assets."
  • "We are passionate about disruption."
  • "Reach out to explore potential opportunities."
