08 / Construction & Clear Space

Geometric, to the degree.

Geometry
Two nested chevrons on a 60° axis. Inner chevron sits at exactly half the outer height — the heritage "VV" read.
Stroke
Single uniform weight, rounded caps and joins. Inner echo holds 55% opacity to signal depth and motion.
Clear space
Minimum margin equals the inner chevron's width on every side. Never crowd the mark.
Minimum size
24px digital / 8mm print. Below this, drop the inner echo for legibility.
