F·01 / Color Tokens

Semantic over raw.

Build with semantic tokens (--bg, --surface, --accent) — never raw hex. The semantic layer remaps automatically between dark and light. Click any token to copy its variable.

F·01.1 Surfaces & text
F·01.2 Accent & status

The brand's discipline rule carries into product: --accent is reserved for the single most important action or value in a view. Status colors (success, warning, info) are functional only — never decorative.

F·02 / Typography

One scale, two families.

Be Vietnam Pro for everything readable — drawn with full Vietnamese diacritics; JetBrains Mono for labels, data, code, and metadata. The scale is fixed — pick a token, don't invent a size.

Xây dựng với tốc độ Việt Nam Vietnamese · ăâđêôơư · áảạệố

Mono is always uppercased with +0.14em–0.2em tracking for labels. Body line-height is 1.5; display drops to 0.92–1.0 with -0.03em tracking.

F·03 / Spacing & Layout

A 4px base.

Every gap, pad, and margin is a multiple of 4. Use the spacing tokens; the grid is 12-column with a 1240px max content width.

F·04 / Radius & Elevation

Tight corners, low light.

Corners stay small — the brand reads precise, not soft. Elevation is restrained: shadows separate layers, they don't decorate.

F·05 / Motion

Fast in, settled.

One easing curve across the system — cubic-bezier(.2,.7,.2,1). Quick entrance, gentle settle. Never bouncy. Hover the cards to feel each duration.
